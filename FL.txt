A. Core Platform & Admin
	•	Multi-branch / multi-entity support
	•	Multiple hospitals, clinics, diagnostic centers
	•	Centralized vs branch-specific configurations
	•	Cross-branch patient lookup
	•	Master Data Management
	•	Departments, units, wards, ICUs, OTs
	•	Services, procedures, packages, tariffs
	•	Item masters (drugs, consumables, assets)
	•	ICD-10 / procedure codes (or configurable code sets)
	•	User Management & Roles
	•	Role-based access control (RBAC)
	•	Page/module level permissions
	•	Department-based access
	•	Maker-checker / approvals
	•	Audit & Security
	•	Audit logs (who did what, when, from where)
	•	IP-based restrictions (optional)
	•	Session management, forced logouts
	•	Data encryption at rest and in transit
	•	Localization & Config
	•	Currency, tax configs (GST / local)
	•	Timezone, date format, language support
	•	Custom fields & forms

B. Patient & Front Office
1. Registration & Demographics
	•	Unique MRN (medical record number)
	•	Multi-ID support (govt ID, insurance ID, corporate ID)
	•	Biometric-based patient identification (finger/iris/face)
	•	Family linking, corporate/TPA linking
	•	Document upload (ID proofs, previous records)
2. Appointment & Scheduling
	•	Doctor-wise slots
	•	Department/Service-wise appointments
	•	Online booking (future web/app integration)
	•	Teleconsultation slot flag
	•	No-show tracking & auto-reminders (SMS/WhatsApp/email)
3. Front Office & Queue Management
	•	Token generation
	•	Doctor/department-wise queues
	•	Real-time display board integration
	•	Priority tagging (emergency/paid priority/VIP)
4. Patient Portal (optional module)
	•	View prescriptions, lab reports, radiology reports
	•	Download invoices
	•	Request appointments, refill prescriptions
	•	Pay bills online

C. Clinical / EMR Modules
1. Outpatient (OPD) EMR
	•	SOAP notes (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan)
	•	Chief complaints, history, allergies
	•	Vitals & BMI
	•	Clinical templates by specialty
	•	e-Prescription (linked with drug database)
	•	Lab & radiology orders from EMR
	•	Follow-up & revisit tracking
2. Inpatient (IPD) Management
	•	Admission/transfer/discharge workflows
	•	Bed management, bed allocation and transfer
	•	Ward/Room-category based tariffs
	•	Clinical notes: progress notes, nursing notes, specialty notes
	•	eMAR (Medication administration record)
3. Nursing Station
	•	Care plans
	•	Vitals charting (with device integration in future)
	•	Intake/output chart
	•	Medication schedules & administration
	•	Procedure scheduling & execution logs
4. OT / OR Management
	•	OT scheduling (surgeon, anesthetist, assistants, theater)
	•	Pre-op checks, consents, checklists
	•	OT utilization tracking
	•	Implants/consumables usage tracking (auto-billing)
	•	Post-op notes & recovery tracking
5. ICU Module
	•	Specialized ICU flows (frequent vitals)
	•	Ventilator parameters (manual entry / integration ready)
	•	Critical care checklists
	•	Sepsis / risk alerts (future decision support)
6. Emergency / Casualty
	•	Quick registration & triage
	•	Triage categories (red/yellow/green)
	•	MLC (medico-legal case) flagging & reporting
	•	Emergency treatment notes
	•	Conversion to IP / OP case

D. Diagnostics & Pharmacy
1. Laboratory Information System (LIS)
	•	Test catalog with panels & profiles
	•	Sample collection & barcode generation
	•	Sample tracking (collected, received, in process, verified)
	•	Machine integration ready (HL7 / ASTM)
	•	Result entry, validation, critical value alerts
	•	Printable and portal-viewable lab reports
2. Radiology / RIS & PACS
	•	Radiology order management
	•	Modality scheduling (X-ray, CT, MRI, USG, etc.)
	•	Integration with PACS (linking images via URL/viewer)
	•	Reporting templates by modality
	•	Second opinion workflows
3. Pharmacy (Inpatient & Retail)
	•	Drug formulary management
	•	Inpatient medication orders from EMR
	•	Bed-side issue & return
	•	Retail pharmacy billing (walk-in & IP/OP-linked)
	•	Stock check at order time
	•	Substitutions and generics
	•	Expiry and narcotics tracking
4. Blood Bank (optional but nice for larger setups)
	•	Donor registration & screening
	•	Blood collection, processing & component separation
	•	Inventory: group, Rh, component-wise
	•	Cross-matching and issue
	•	Traceability logs

1. Inventory & Procurement

	•	Stock management per store/sub-store

F. Finance & Revenue Cycle
1. Billing & Tariffs
	•	OP billing (consults, procedures, services)
	•	IP billing (room charges, nursing, procedures, pharmacy, diagnostics)

	•	Approval & enhancement tracking
	•	Claims preparation & submission tracking
	•	Denial & rejection tracking
3. Cash & Payments

	•	Refunds & credit notes
	•	Daily cash closure reports
4. Accounting Interface
	•	Mapping to chart of accounts
	•	Export to Tally/ERP or via API
	•	Revenue recognition by department/service

G. HR & Biometric Module
1. HR Basics
	•	Staff profiles (doctors, nurses, admin, technicians)
	•	Department assignment, designations
	•	Qualifications, licenses, certifications
	•	Shift & duty roster management
	•	Leave management (apply, approve, track)
2. Biometric Integrations
You can treat biometrics as a horizontal capability that connects to multiple modules:
	•	Attendance & Access Control
	•	Staff attendance via biometric devices at entry/exit
	•	Late/early, overtime calculation
	•	Access control for sensitive areas (ICU, OT, pharmacy, server room)
	•	Device sync (cloud-based, scheduled polling)
	•	Patient Identification
	•	Biometric capture at registration (fingerprint/face, depending on region regulatory comfort)
	•	Fast patient lookup at front office, pharmacy, sample collection
	•	Clinical & Legal Sign-offs
	•	Biometric-authenticated e-signatures for:
	•	OT notes
	•	Discharge summaries
	•	Critical care orders
	•	High-risk medication orders
	•	Audit trail: “Which doctor actually signed this?”
	•	Device Layer Design
	•	Support for multiple device brands via adapters/SDKs
	•	Local agent that talks to on-prem devices and syncs to cloud
	•	Failover mechanisms:
	•	Fallback to PIN/OTP when biometric fails
	•	Offline caching and later sync

H. Quality, Compliance & Risk
	•	Incident reporting (falls, medication errors, near-misses)

	•	Checklists (OT safety checklist, ICU checklist, infection control)
	•	Consent management:

	•	Digital signature capture (stylus + biometric optional)
	•	MLC management (for countries where this matters)

J. Integration Layer & APIs
Design this as a first-class module, not an afterthought.
	•	Standard interfaces
	•	HL7/FHIR readiness (for future EMR/EHR exchanges)
	•	DICOM worklist and PACS integration points
	•	Lab equipment integration
	•	Payment gateways (Razorpay/Stripe/etc.)
	•	SMS/WhatsApp/email gateways
	•	External Systems
	•	Existing ERPs (SAP, Oracle, Tally, etc.)
	•	Third-party LIS/RIS if hospital already has them
	•	Government systems / national health IDs (if applicable later)
	•	Developer-friendly APIs
	•	RESTful APIs for all major entities (patients, appointments, bills, labs, pharmacy)
	•	Webhooks for events (bill created, discharge completed, report ready, etc.)
